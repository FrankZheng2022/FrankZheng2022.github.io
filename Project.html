Using supervised learning and unsupervised learning algorithms to analyze and detect fake news

We have all witnessed the huge impacts of fake news spread through social media during the 2016 U.S. presidential election. Based on the research conducted by the European Research Council, about one in four Americans viewed at least one fake news article during the 2016 presidential campaign season. In this project, we will be using the data science toolbox to analyze and detect fake news claims in "Liar" Dataset, as proposed by William Yang Wang in 2017.

First, we will apply various unsupervised learning algorithms such as topic modelling, doc2vec embedding, as well as TSNE dimensional reduction to explore and visualize the fake news dataset. Then based on the doc2vec embedding, we will apply supervised learning algorithms such as k nearest neighbors, support vector machine, random forest, and neural network to classify the fake news. In fact, our neural network model achieves a much higher accuracy than the results posted by the original paper !!!
